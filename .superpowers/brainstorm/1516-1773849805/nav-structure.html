C — 5-Tab Bar

Full tab bar: Home, Charge, Eat, Rewards, Profile. Every major destination one tap away. Stay and Shop still accessible via carousel. Heaviest navigation but maximum discoverability.

My recommendation: Option A (3-tab) — keeps the carousel as the hero interaction and matches the cinematic, minimal feel of the brand. Rewards and Profile are the only tabs users need outside of it.
